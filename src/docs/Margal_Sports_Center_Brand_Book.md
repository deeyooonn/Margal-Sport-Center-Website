## 3. Typography Hierarchy

To match the energetic and bold aesthetic of the Margal Sports Center logo, use the following font pairings for marketing materials and social media graphics:

* **Primary Display/Headline Font:** **Oswald** or **Impact**
  * *Usage:* Big promotional headers, rate prices (e.g., "₱400", "RATE UPDATE"), and attention-grabbing text. Use in ALL CAPS.
* **Secondary/Body Font:** **Inter** or **Segoe UI**
  * *Usage:* Long-form post descriptions, address details, and contact information. Provides maximum readability on mobile devices.
